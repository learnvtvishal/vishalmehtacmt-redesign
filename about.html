Vishal Mehta is a Certified Market Technician (CMT) from the USA and an MS in Finance graduate with over 24 years of deep market experience spanning institutional research, trading education, and algorithmic systems.

He has trained professionals at the Reserve Bank of India and central banks across South Asia.

As a trader, he actively trades equity and commodity options on BSE, NSE and MCX — identifying quantifiable, rule-based edges that work across market cycles.

As an educator, he has mentored 5,000+ professionals and over 10,000 traders — helping them build process-driven systems that truly hold up.
